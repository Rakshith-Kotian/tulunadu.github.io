About Tulunadu

Tulunadu is a region along the southwestern coast of India, known for its rich traditions, vibrant festivals, and the Tulu language. This land is home to Yakshagana, Bhoota Kola, Kambala, and a variety of unique customs that have been preserved for centuries.

This website aims to be a complete resource about Tulunadu — its history, language, art, food, and people. Whether you are from Tulunadu or simply curious, you will find something to love here.
