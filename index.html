Welcome to Tulunadu

Tulunadu is a culturally rich coastal region known for the Tulu language, Yakshagana, Bhoota Kola and Kambala. This site celebrates the people, festivals and traditions of the region — shown here under the traditional maroon field with the sun and moon.
