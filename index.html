Welcome

Tulunadu is a vibrant coastal region in southwestern India, rich in language, deep-rooted traditions, and unique cultural heritage. Home to the Tulu-speaking community, this land treasures Yakshagana, Bhoota Kola, Kambala, and a cuisine bursting with flavors. Here, the warmth of the sun and the calm of the moon meet the greenery of nature.
