Bhoota Kola

A spiritual ritual involving dance, music, and elaborate costumes, Bhoota Kola is performed to honor guardian spirits and seek their blessings for the community.

Nagamandala

A unique serpent worship ritual involving vibrant art drawn on the ground using colored powders, accompanied by devotional songs and performances.

Kambala

A traditional buffalo race held in water-filled paddy fields during the farming season, symbolizing agricultural heritage and community spirit.
